DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

Vision Public School was started with an aim to make our students a better citizens and to utilize their education in building a better society. At Vision Public School, we teach our students to follow their heart and encouraging them to achieve their goal. Along with education, we want our students to get involved in sports, extra curricular activities etc. Innovation is another important focus of our school and we encourage students to convert their ideas into real time applications.
